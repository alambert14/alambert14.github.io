Setting Up Your Environment

Eclipse is the IDE (integrated development environment) required by FIRST for Java programming. "But why can't I use BlueJ, or *insert other IDE*," you might ask. First of all, Eclipse is an incredibly common Java IDE, and you will probably see it again in college and the professional world. It is good to expand your horizons. Secondly, FIRST creates plugins specifically for Eclipse so that programming the robot can be much easier.

These plugins include a variety of utilities (described further in "Using Eclipse"):

  • Templates for different robot design
  • Use of the WPILib libraries

The installation of Eclipse is also very well documented (I repeat, WITH PICTURES) in the WPI Screen Steps. Follow them to a tee and you will be all set.
